# Privacy Policy

**Go Inward Out**
**Effective Date:** April 2026
**Last Updated:** April 2026

## Overview

Go Inward Out is a personal journaling and AI-assisted reflection app for inner experiences. Your privacy is foundational to this app. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

## What Data Is Collected

### Data You Create
- **Journal entries**: Text you write or dictate about your inner experiences (dreams, meditations, etc.)
- **Voice recordings**: Audio captured during voice-to-text entry (stored on your device only)
- **Profile information**: Optional birth data, inner life profile responses, and preferences you choose to share
- **Reflections and responses**: Answers to prompts and questions within the app

### Data Stored on Your Device
All of the above is stored locally on your device using Core Data (Apple's on-device database). Your journal entries, audio recordings, profile data, and app settings never leave your device unless you explicitly request AI analysis or enable iCloud sync.

### Data Sent to AI (Only When You Request It)
When you choose to use AI-powered features (readings, synthesis, text cleanup), the relevant text is sent to the Anthropic API (Claude) for processing. Specifically:
- **Readings**: The text of the experience you request a reading for
- **Synthesis**: Aggregated themes from your recent experiences
- **Text cleanup**: The text you ask to be refined
- **Metadata extraction**: Entry text for automatic title/type suggestions

**Important**: AI analysis is always initiated by you. Your data is never sent automatically or in the background. You control when and whether AI features are used.

Per Anthropic's API Terms of Service, data sent via the API is not used to train AI models and is not retained beyond the processing window.

## What Is NOT Collected

- No third-party analytics or tracking SDKs
- No advertising SDKs or ad identifiers
- No crash reporting services that transmit data to third parties
- No usage telemetry sent to our servers
- No social media integrations
- No location data

## iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync, your journal data is synced across your Apple devices via Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. This data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple. Audio recordings are not included in iCloud sync due to size constraints.

## Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:
- **Individual entries**: Swipe to delete or use the edit screen
- **All data**: Delete the app from your device to remove all local data
- **iCloud data**: Disabling iCloud sync or deleting the app removes synced data

## Data Export

You can export all your data at any time in PDF, CSV, or JSON format via Settings > Export Codex. Your data is yours.

## Children's Privacy

Go Inward Out is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Significant changes will be communicated through the app.
